A Quick Favor

Loved your shoot? Leave us a review.

Reviews are how other Sarasota realtors, agents, and homeowners find us. A 30-second Google review goes a long way for a small studio — and we read every one.

Leave a Google Review  →

Opens Google's 5-star review form in a new tab. No account creation needed if you're already signed into Google.

How It Works

Three steps, thirty seconds.

  1. Tap the button above. It opens Google's review form for LF Property Media.
  2. Pick a star rating and write a sentence or two about your experience. Anything is helpful — the editing, the turnaround, the result.
  3. Hit Post. That's it. We'll see it within the hour and very likely respond personally to thank you.

No Google account? You'll need a free one to leave a review — it takes a minute to make and works for every other Google service too.
